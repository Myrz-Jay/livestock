Common Questions

How quickly can you respond?

During the day we are usually out on jobs or in rural areas with poor reception, this may not always be possible, please text or fill out the contact sheet and we will get back to you. We will aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! All quotes are free and come with no obligation. We'll provide detailed pricing based on your needs.

What's your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Please give us 48 hours notice for regular services, 7 days for events.

Do you travel to remote areas?

Yes, we service properties up to 150km from the Central Coast. Travel fees will apply.
