Frequently Asked Questions

We service Greater Sydney, Hunter Valley, Central Coast, Blue Mountains, and surrounding regions up to 350km from Sydney. Travel fees may apply for distant locations.

We can set up under cover if you have a garage, veranda, or covered area. For light rain, we have gazebos. Heavy rain may require rescheduling - we'll work with you to find a solution.

Yes, we have $20 million public liability insurance. All our staff have Working with Children checks and are fully trained in animal handling and safety procedures.

We typically need a flat area of about 5m x 5m minimum. We can adapt to different spaces - driveways, backyards, parks, or halls work well.

Please inform us of any allergies when booking. We provide hand sanitizer and can arrange for children with allergies to enjoy watching from a safe distance.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and school holidays. Popular dates fill quickly!
